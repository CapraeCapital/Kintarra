01 · Our Thesis

A patient roll up of the IT services that never go out of demand. The market is large and deeply fragmented, full of excellent companies built by founders who now want a partner that can carry the work further than it could alone.

01

Buy with respect

We pursue a single great company at a time. Profitable, growing, and trusted by its clients. We have sat in the founder seat and we make the process clear, fair, and calm.

02

Connect without erasing

Brands and teams stay intact. We share back office strength, security standards, and demand generation across the group so each company keeps its identity and gains real leverage.

03

Build for the long term

We are operators first. We hold for the long run, reinvest in talent and tooling, and measure success by client retention and the legacy each company carries forward.

04

Stay essential and recurring

Managed IT, cybersecurity, and secure data handling are demand that never disappears, giving the group durable revenue and clients a partner they can rely on for decades.

02 · Fully Managed IT Services

Services across the full technology lifecycle.

Kintarra handles all of your IT services needs while you stay focused on your goals. You get a custom IT support solution built for your business from the services below.

Service 01

IT Asset Management

What we deliver

Managing your IT assets efficiently is crucial to maximizing their value and minimizing costs. Our services cover the entire asset lifecycle, from procurement and deployment to maintenance and disposal, keeping track of hardware and software, ensuring license compliance, and optimizing utilization to streamline operations.

Service 02

IT Physical Disposal

What we deliver

Businesses generate more data than ever, and when it is time to destroy excess, old, or duplicate data, how that is carried out is as important to the security of the organization as its systems for data access. Kintarra fully comprehends the specific needs, sensitivities, and risks of destroying your and your clients' IT assets.

Service 03

Data Center Cleaning & Consulting

What we deliver

Protecting the health of the personnel and equipment in your data center is central to your ongoing success, and with modern design practically any downtime is unacceptable. We offer server room cleaning and consulting so your data center and employees are not impacted by airborne or debris-related issues that interrupt operations.

Service 04

Remote IT Services

What we deliver

With Kintarra's remote IT services, you gain a team of dedicated professionals ready to address your IT needs from anywhere. We diagnose, resolve, and manage issues without onsite visits, allowing for flexible, efficient problem-solving that keeps downtime to a minimum while delivering expert support tailored to you.
